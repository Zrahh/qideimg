This Multiple Grip Pull-Up Bar has one center bar extending into a handle on each side and a set of parallel bars.This bar is suitable for concrete/brick walls. Many different pull-up grips are available with these available surfaces depending on which muscle groups you want to work. Mounting at a height just at the tips of your fingers allows you to do pull-ups without touching the ground. Additionally, the center bar is perfect for use as a mounting place for resistance bands.

Details:
- 3 different bars for many positions
- Foam grips for added comfort
- High quality steel construction
- Perform a variety of upper body exercises from wide-narrow grip pull-ups to hanging leg raises
- Can be used as a mount for exercise and resistance straps and bands
- Includes mounting hardware and instructions
